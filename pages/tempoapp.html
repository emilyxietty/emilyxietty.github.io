Tempo is a debate timer with multiple functions that will enhance your user experience.

Debate Formats:
- High School British Parliamentary (5 mins)
- Standard British Parliamentary (7 mins)
- Canadian Parliamentary (7 mins)
- World School's Debate Format (8 mins)


Time Ring and Vibrate:
💥 1 time when protected time begins and ends
💥 2 times when grace time begins
💥 3 times when time ends


Timer Colours:
🟦 blue: protected time
🟩 green: non-protected time (POIs allowed)
🟥 red: grace time
⬛️ black: grace time over


Timer Functionalities:
⏯ Start/Pause
🔄 Restart
↔️ Fast-forward/-backward 10 seconds
⏭ Swipe next for next speaker in debate


App Functionalities:
💭 random motion generator
💬 information on the debating formats offered
