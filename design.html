CAISA Fashion Show

Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, InDesign, Illustrator
CAISA Fashion Show (CFS) is the largest student-run charity fashion show in Canada. We have raised over $380,000 on behalf of Children's Health Foundation. I have been on the team for the past 4 years, first as a Graphics Manager (2018-19), then Graphics Director (2019-20; 2020-21), and finally, Creative Director (2021-22). On this team, I have been in charge of the graphics for all creative and promotional events, including the final show and website graphics.

Every year, this team produces several lookbooks compiled by the Graphics Team.
